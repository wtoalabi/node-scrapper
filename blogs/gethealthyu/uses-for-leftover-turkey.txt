It’s a lot of work to be the host, but anyone who does it time and time again knows there’s something so special and rewarding about it. But when the holiday is over and done with, all those leftovers, like the remnants of that beautiful bird, can take up valuable real estate in your refrigerator and freezer. So what to do? The following seven recipes will let you get the most out of your leftover turkey while freeing up space in your kitchen.
