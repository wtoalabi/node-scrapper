"Salads are a no-brainer in summer. Crunchy, cold and light, who doesn’t love digging into a delicious salad with the sun on your back? But winter is a different story. A bowl of raw veggies don’t seem so appealing when you’re covered in layers and craving soups, roasts and slow cooker recipes. However, there’s no rule book regarding what can, or cannot, go into your salad, so why not reinvent your bowl of healthy goodness to coincide with the time of year and your mood? That’s where warm winter salads come in. This yummy salad combines butternut squash with a hearty helping of quinoa to really give your salad some healthy and filling bulk. For those who like a little spice in your life, the Warm Chipotle Lime Sweet Potato Salad is the perfect option for you.\n\n\nWild Rice Orzo Cranberry Salad by A Healthy Life For Me\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nThis Wild Rice Orzo Cranberry Salad is a colorful concoction of incredibly filling and earthy ingredients.
