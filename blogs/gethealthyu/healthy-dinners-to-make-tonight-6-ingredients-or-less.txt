Score.\nBruschetta Baked Chicken\nThis recipe has summer written all over it. Fresh garden tomatoes and freshly picked basil are the best way to make this chicken!\n\n\n\n\n\n\n3 tablespoons olive oil, divided\n2 campari tomatoes, diced and divided\nFresh basil\nSalt & pepper, to taste\n2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts\n3/4 cup mozzarella cheese, shredded\n\nPreheat the oven to 400°. Combine the olive oil, tomatoes and basil in a small bowl. Place chicken breasts in a baking pan sprinkle with salt and pepper. Spoon tomato mixture over each breast and top with mozzarella cheese. Bake for 20 minutes or until cooked through.
