"It doesn’t get simpler than meals in a mug. Toss all the ingredients in, give it a little stir, pop it in the microwave and voila! This delicious variety of easy meals in a mug takes just minutes to make, but tastes like you’ve been at it all day! From breakfast to dessert, these twelve single serving sensations will leave your belly full and you with very little clean up!
