"Ingredients
  
  1 cup unsweetened vanilla almond milk
  1 cup soft tofu (aka: silken)
  1 scoop vanilla plant protein powder
  ¼ ripe avocado
  2 pitted dates
  1 tsp vanilla extract
  Ice
  
  Directions
