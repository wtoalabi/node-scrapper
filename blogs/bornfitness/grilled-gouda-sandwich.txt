"This elevated grilled cheese sandwich gives you all the comfort of the original. With its sweet and savory flavor, you would never guess there is 17g of fiber!\nIngredients\n\n2 slices Dave’s Killer Bread 21 Whole Grains and Seeds\n2 strips uncured turkey bacon\n1/2 ripe pear, thinly sliced\n2 handfuls raw spinach\n¼ avocado smashed\n½ Tbsp dijon mustard\n1-2 slice gouda cheese\n\nElevated Grilled Cheese Directions\nPrepare the “inside”\n\nCook turkey bacon in a pan. Remove bacon,  set aside.\nIn the same pan cook raw spinach and sliced pears 30-40 seconds (leaves will wilt and pears become tender). Remove, set aside.\n\nBuild it\n\nSmear smashed avocado and dijon on bread.\nTop with cooked bacon, pears, spinach, and cheese.\nIn the same pan, grill both sides until bread is toasted and cheese is melted.\n\n \n\nNatalie SabinNatalie is a M.S. in Nutrition & Performance candidate; anticipated completion December 2021.
