"Yields: 4 servings\nPrep Time: 10 minutes\nCook Time: 40 minutes\nWhat Is A Hobo Foil Packet?\nA hobo foil packet is a quick, simple meal solution popular among campers. It’s a bundle of cut-up ingredients that are packed into some foil and thrown on top of the campfire. Don’t have a campfire? No sweat. Let your kids make their own foil pack and pop ’em in your oven for a fun and super simple family night meal.\nSausage And Peppers On Sourdough Crostini Ingredients\n\n1 pound chicken or turkey sausage\n2 red bell peppers, sliced\n2 green bell peppers, sliced\n1 large sweet onion\n8 Crimini mushrooms, sliced\nThyme, fresh sprigs\nDried Oregano\nSourdough bread\nMarinara sauce\nFoil\n\nHobo Foil Packet Directions\n\nPreheat oven to 375.\nRemove sausage from casing. Section sausage into quarter-size balls (they don’t have to be perfectly round).\nPlace sausage, peppers, onion, mushrooms, 1-2 sprigs of thyme, and a dash of oregano on a 10″ foil sheet.\nTop with a second sheet of foil and fold edges together to create a packet. Makes about 4 hobo packs.\nPlace packs on a baking sheet. Cook for 35 minutes.\n\nNOTE: Do not open packets to check them. Removing the steam will slow the cooking process.\nSourdough Crostini Directions\n\nPlace 1 tablespoon oil in a pan on medium heat.\nToast each side of sourdough bread.\nServe sausage and peppers on toast with a side of sauce.\n\n \n\nNatalie SabinNatalie is a M.S. in Nutrition & Performance candidate; anticipated completion December 2021.
