"The perfect game day appetizer. Pair these crispy taders with your favorite brisket and roasted root vegetable for a full meal.\nIngredients\n\n2 lbs mini round potatoes (Sometimes called “one bite” potatoes)\n2 Tbsp olive oil\n3 cups baby arugula (you can always add more greens!)\n2 Tbsp reduced-fat feta cheese\nCoarse Salt\n\nDirections\n\nIn a large pot add potatoes and cover with water. Bring to a boil. Cook until potatoes are tender (can poke easily with a fork).\nDrain and sit for 3 minutes to cool slightly.\nLine a baking sheet with parchment paper then scatter cooked potatoes around the paper.\nUsing a flat mallet (or whatever flat utensil you have), press into each potato to smash it flat.\nDrizzle olive oil and course salt on potatoes. Bake for 20-30 minutes at 425 degrees until edges are crispy with a tender center.\nTop with ranch and buffalo sauces (recipes below), arugula, and feta cheese.\n\n \nThe Sauce Ingredients\n\n2 cups non-fat plain Greek yogurt\n1 packet Hidden Valley Ranch dry seasoning\nHot sauce or buffalo wing sauce\n\nRanch Sauce\nCombine 1 cup plain non-fat Greek yogurt and 1/2 to 1 Tbsp hidden Valley Ranch Dry seasoning. Adjust for desired taste.\nBuffalo Sauce\nCombine 1 cup plain non-fat Greek yogurt with your 1/2-1 Tbsp of your favorite hot sauce or buffalo sauce. Adjust for desired spice level.\n\nNatalie SabinNatalie is a M.S. in Nutrition & Performance candidate; anticipated completion December 2021.
