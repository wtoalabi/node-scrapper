"This is an easy dessert you can whip together all year long. Use any fruit you have on hand—strawberries, peaches, blueberries, and mangos work great.\nFrozen Yogurt Sandwich Ingredients:\nYields: 8 servings\n\n16 graham cracker squares\n1 cup non-fat Greek yogurt\n1 cup whipped cream\n1 cup strawberries, diced\n1-2 tsp vanilla extract\n\nDirections:\n\nLine baking sheet with parchment or wax paper.\nLine up 8 graham cracker squares on paper.\nIn a bowl combine yogurt, whipped cream, strawberries, and vanilla extract.\nScoop onto graham crackers.\nTop each with a second graham cracker square to make sandwiches.\nPromptly place baking sheet in the freezer (move quickly so the mixture doesn’t run off).\nFreeze for 1-2 hours.\nEnjoy!\n\nNote: if you freeze longer, no problem!
