"Yields 4 servings\nIngredients\n\n1 box pasta, cooked\n2-3 crowns broccoli, chopped\n4-5 cloves garlic, chopped\n1 lemon\n2 Tbsp olive oil\n2-3 Tbsp Parmesan, freshly grated\nSalt\n\nDirections\nIf you are newer to cooking and not used to “timing” the dish so it comes out at the same time, make the pasta salad FIRST, then do the scallops last.\nMaking The Pasta Salad\n\nBring a pot of water to a boil.\nBlanch broccoli for 3 minutes. Remove and run cold water over it to stop the cooking process.\nIn a small saucepan sauté garlic, oil, and zest of 1 lemon for 3 minutes.\nRemove from heat and add the juice of one lemon.\nCombine cooked pasta, broccoli, garlic lemon sauce, a few pinches of salt, and cheese in a bowl. Mix.\n\nNote: add a little more lemon and oil at the end if needed\nSearing The Scallops\nSear in a hot pan with 1 tablespoon butter. 3 minutes on each side.\n\nNatalie SabinNatalie is a M.S. in Nutrition & Performance candidate; anticipated completion December 2021.
