"Stumped for lunch ideas while you are remote working?\nWe have more clients than ever working remotely. That means fewer lunch outings with coworkers and more meals prepared at home.\nMake your lunch in minutes. No cooking skills needed.\nIngredients\n\nPrecooked rotisserie chicken (how easy is that?), skin removed and chopped\n1 cup Kalamata olives, chopped\n1 cup red onion, thinly sliced\n1 package reduced-fat feta cheese, crumbled\n1 bunch baby arugula\n2-3 small plum tomato, diced\nSmall tortilla shells (flour or corn)\nOptional dressing: plain greek yogurt + fresh chopped dill + lemon juice dressing + S&P\n\nDirections\n\nIn a small skillet on low heat toss together 2 handfuls chicken + a small handful each of: olives, raw onion + feta + baby arugula.\nCombine and cook for 2 minutes until the feta starts to melt and the olives warm slightly.\nScoop the warm mixture into toasted tortillas.\nTop with fresh tomatoes, more arugula, and homemade dressing.\nEnjoy!\n\n\nNatalie SabinNatalie is a M.S. in Nutrition & Performance candidate; anticipated completion December 2021.
