"If you love dips and sauce, this is a recipe you will want to put in rotation.\nTahini is a paste made from sesame seeds. That means high in monounsaturated and polyunsaturated fats, making it a “healthy” fat. Tahini is rich in B vitamins, Vitamin E, and several beneficial minerals.\nTop this sauce on steamed greens—collards, kale, and broccoli. Make it a rice bowl and add chicken or keep it vegan with tempeh.\nTahini Garlic Lemon Sauce\nYields: 8 servings\n\n1-3 cloves garlic (think of this like level of heat. 1 is mild, 3 is very strong but so good!)\n1 cup curly parsley\n1 lemon, juiced seeds removed\n1 1/3 cup water\n1 cup Tahini\nFew pinches of salt\n\nHow To\nCombine ingredients into a food processor or high-power blender. Mix until smooth.\n\nNatalie SabinNatalie is a M.S. in Nutrition & Performance candidate; anticipated completion December 2021.
